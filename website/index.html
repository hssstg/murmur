Features

01

Hold to speak

Hold any hotkey to record, release to transcribe. Text appears instantly at your cursor — no copy-paste, no window switching.

02

Works in any app

Text is typed directly into whatever app you're using — browsers, notes, email, chat, code editors. No compatibility issues.

03

AI cleanup

Optionally connect an AI to automatically remove filler words, fix mishearing, and clean up punctuation after each transcription.

04

History & Corrections

All transcriptions are saved locally. Edit any entry and keep both the original and corrected version for your own reference.

05

Gets smarter over time

Murmur learns from your corrections and suggests words to add to your vocabulary, so it recognises your terms more accurately.

06

Your hotkey, your way

Option, Control, CapsLock, F-keys, or mouse side buttons — map any key to push-to-talk. Optionally remap a mouse button to Enter.

How it works

Up and running
in three steps

STEP 01

Grant permissions

System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility. Add Murmur to the allow list so it can listen for your hotkey and type into other apps. Grant microphone access when prompted.

STEP 02

Choose your hotkey

Click the menu bar icon → Settings. Pick any hotkey — Option, Control, CapsLock, F-keys, or mouse buttons. Optionally connect an LLM to clean up your text.

STEP 03

Hold and speak

Place your cursor anywhere. Hold ⌥ Right Option, speak, release. Your words appear instantly — no extra clicks.
