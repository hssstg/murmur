Rebuilt from scratch as a lightweight native Mac app. Faster, smaller, and works in every app including terminals and code editors.

  • Text appears instantly at your cursor — no window switching, no copy-paste
  • Optional AI cleanup to remove filler words and fix mishearing
  • Custom vocabulary to improve accuracy for your own words and names
  • All transcription history saved locally on your Mac
  • Menu bar icon with quick access to history, vocabulary, and settings
  • Remap a mouse button to Enter for quick confirmation
