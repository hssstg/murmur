Release Notes

Changelog

v2.0
April 9, 2026

Fully offline. Rebuilt speech recognition with SenseVoice — no cloud, no account, no internet required. First public open-source release.

  • Offline ASR via SenseVoice — Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, Cantonese
  • Built-in punctuation and inverse text normalization
  • Capsule floating window with real-time waveform visualization
  • LLM polish with "Refining..." status display
  • Usage statistics dashboard — 30-day charts, hourly distribution, recognition quality
  • AI hotword extraction from dictation history
  • Developer ID signed for Gatekeeper compatibility
  • Open sourced under MIT license on GitHub
v0.2.0
March 3, 2026

Rebuilt from scratch as a lightweight native Mac app. Faster, smaller, and works in every app including terminals and code editors.

  • Text appears instantly at your cursor — no window switching, no copy-paste
  • Optional AI cleanup to remove filler words and fix mishearing
  • Custom vocabulary to improve accuracy for your own words and names
  • All transcription history saved locally on your Mac
  • Menu bar icon with quick access to history, vocabulary, and settings
  • Remap a mouse button to Enter for quick confirmation
v0.1.0
February 24, 2026

First working version. Proof of concept.
